STIMULUS INTEGRATED SERVICES TRADING AND CONTRACTING

Welcome to SISCO Company website. We are a leading company located in State of Qatar serving the Trading, Information Technology , Cleaning ,Contracting, HVAC, MEP, civil, interior works and building material supply of the country. Our track records of quality, safety and reliability have made it a contractor of choice for major developers, government ministries, and private clients in Qatar.

Our Objectives

Since establishment, we have set clear objectives to differentiate ourselves in the Qatar Construction and Contracting industry through high-quality and timely delivery standards of several integrated specialized services.

Our engineering resources are readily available to respond promptly to your inquiries and start immediately with a thorough understanding of your needs and requirements.

Our Employees will promptly assume responsibility for the process and take you through the engineering stage, planning, quality requirements, and execution.

Scope of Work

SISCO has a team of professionals who have successfully completed numerous medium-scale projects for many prestigious clients in different sectors including construction, refurbishment & maintenance of:

• Private Villas & Housing Units.
• Multi-Story Apartment Buildings.
• Multi-Story Hotel & Hotel Apartment Buildings.
• Housing Compounds.
• Retail & General Service Shops.
• Industrial Buildings.
• Steel Structures.

In addition to the above mentioned works, SISCO has an independent IT division, offering various ERP Solutions for Freight and Logistics , Retail, Manufacturing , Hospitality and Healthcare sector . We also offer Interior Design solutions. We have a dedicated team for this service with the capability of taking both internal and external independent projects. .
